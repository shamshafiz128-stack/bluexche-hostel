    Policies overview

    Short rules — so booking stays fair for everyone.

    1. Occupancy

      Each booking is 1 room for a maximum of 2 guests. Extra guests are not allowed.

    2. Check-in / check-out

      Check-in from 2:00 PM. Check-out by 11:00 AM next day.

    3. Rates

      Daily rent and meal prices are fixed — same rate every day of the week. No weekend surge.

    4. ID & booking

      Valid CNIC or passport required at reception. Booking date must match your arrival day.

    5. Cancellation

      Free cancel up to 24 hours before arrival. Later cancels keep one day’s rent.

    6. House rules

      No smoking indoors. Quiet hours 11:00 PM – 7:00 AM. Visitors only in lobby.
